Work

Follow the Rhythm

“These Nights” by Avicii is a very popular upbeat song with a stronger underlying meaning than expected. I focused on the chorus of the song as I find it very inspiring to relate to and feel many others do too as the lyrics remind us to live in the moment and not to focus on the small worries of everyday life. The lively, youthful tone of the song is reflected in my poster as it is full of colours and pictures to make it visually appealing to the eye.

About Me

Hello! My name is Sarah. I am a former Ulster University Foundation Art and Design student who is now in first year at Ulster again studying Interaction Design. I began my journey to my career in 2019 through the Foundation Art and Design degree as I did not know what path I wanted to venture into. I instantly fell in love with IXD and knew I wanted to spend the most exciting years of my life in this fast-pace, ever-growing industry.

My chosen manifesto, “Focus on the step in front of you, not the entire staircase” helps to remind me that the journey is equally as important as the final destination. It helps to remind me to create what you are passionate about’ even if you do not know how the final product will look. This manifesto has been really motivational to me because it makes the impossible possible.
